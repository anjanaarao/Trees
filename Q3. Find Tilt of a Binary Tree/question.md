## 3: Find Tilt of a Binary Tree

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Tags:** binary-tree, recursion  

**Problem Reference:**  
- Platform: LeetCode  
- Link: https://leetcode.com/problems/binary-tree-tilt/  

**Concept to Read:**  
- https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/tilt-of-binary-tree/  

**Issue Description:**  
The tilt of a node is the absolute difference between sums of left and right subtrees.  
This problem strengthens subtree sum reasoning.

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Correct total tilt  
- Handles null nodes  
- Time Complexity: O(n)  
- Space Complexity: O(h)
