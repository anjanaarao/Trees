## 2: Sum of Left Leaves

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Tags:** binary-tree, recursion  

**Problem Reference:**  
- Platform: LeetCode  
- Link: https://leetcode.com/problems/sum-of-left-leaves/  

**Concept to Read:**  
- https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/find-sum-left-leaves-binary-tree/  

**Issue Description:**  
Calculate the sum of all left leaf nodes in a binary tree.  
Encourages identifying node roles (left vs right, leaf vs non-leaf).

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
- Correct sum calculation  
- Handles empty tree  
- Time Complexity: O(n)  
- Space Complexity: O(h)
